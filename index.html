About Dakota’s Vending

Dakota’s Vending delivers premium vape products through smart vending machines installed at bars, clubs, and entertainment venues across South Dakota. Each unit features secure ID scanning, credit card payments, and 24/7 access to your favorite brands.

How It Works

  1. Scan your ID to verify age (21+)
  2. Choose your vape product
  3. Pay with a card or mobile wallet
  4. Grab your product instantly — no staff required
